## Full Description (max 4000 chars)
🎵 **BeatFlow** — Your music, your way.

BeatFlow is a beautiful, powerful offline music player designed for music lovers who want the best listening experience from their own collection.

**✨ Features**

🎧 **High-Quality Playback**
- Supports MP3, FLAC, AAC, M4A, OGG, WAV, OPUS, WMA
- Gapless playback for uninterrupted listening
- Background playback with lock screen controls
- Notification player with album art

🎚️ **Equalizer & Audio Effects**
- 5-band graphic equalizer
- 8 built-in presets: Rock, Pop, Classical, Jazz, Dance, Electronic, Hip-Hop
- Custom EQ settings you can save
- Bass boost, virtualizer, reverb controls

📚 **Smart Library**
- Auto-scans your entire music collection
- Organized by Songs, Albums, Artists, Folders
- Recently played & most played smart lists
- Instant search across your library

📋 **Playlists**
- Create unlimited custom playlists
- Smart auto-playlists: Favorites, Recently Played, Most Played
- Drag-to-reorder queue

🎨 **Beautiful Design**
- Dark & light themes
- 8 accent color options
- Smooth animations throughout
- Album art everywhere

🔒 **100% Offline & Private**
- No internet required
- No account needed
- No ads, ever
- Your music never leaves your device
